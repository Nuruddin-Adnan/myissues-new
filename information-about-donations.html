Information about donations

Giving back always leaves you feeling good when you know it will be a worthy cause. Altruism is good for our health, and studies have proven that spending money on others can lower our blood pressure. Helping others activates the reward centers in your brain, so are you ready to give back in a meaningful way while making a profound impact?

We believe that supporting others creates a sense of belonging and makes the world a better place for everyone. Our mission has been to empower people to live their best life, and we have several causes that are requesting your support.

The donations that are made will aid the website users in funding their ads for the requested amounts. Food requests indicate a specific dollar amount that each organizer requires and a brief description of their funding needs. We provide a platform where these ads can run successfully by helping to collect the necessary funds.

Regular Donations

We accept prepaid credit cards sold at your local convenience stores for donations to community members.

Your financial assistance will allow us to meet the ad poster’s food bank purchases. We can support individuals and families to meet their custom needs through your generous donations. Each family is unique, and we make an effort to cater to each user’s specific needs.

Food Bank Donations

Foodbank purchases are beneficial to the community members.

We are experiencing high inflation rates, and food prices are increasing rapidly, making it harder for families to afford the necessities. Income is not growing, but the expenses are rising by the day. Your support helps families navigate the economic instability since some people may have lost their jobs.

Also, some people are living on low wages that cannot keep up with the high grocery bills. We seek to get their grocery bills covered. Website users have selected food items from the food bank grocery store and updated the groceries they would need once a week. After choosing the essential groceries from the food bank store, their ad will appear with the funding details.

View the foodbank page to see the number of people registered for assistance at the foodbank. If you would like to donate to a person registered in the foodbank, this page will allow you to direct your funds to a specific person and their family.

Change is possible when people come together to fight a common cause. When you join our work to serve others, we can create immense local value for the community. People can feel hopeful about their future, and they can look to enhance the quality of their lives when their basic needs are met. We request your support to fund our users as every dollar makes a difference. Let us leave the world a better place than when we found it.

Thank you for your continued support and donations. Our programs are not possible without you.
